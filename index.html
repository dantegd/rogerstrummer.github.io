A penniless tango-dancing phd student

I am currently a PhD student and research assistant at Visualization for Decision Making Lab at Stevens Institute of Technology working with professor Ramirez-Marquez. My main research area is in novel mathematical models, machine learning and data visualization techniques for social media and text content analysis.

I did my Masters Degree in High Performance Computing at the Edinburgh Parallel Computer Center at the University of Edinburgh.

I have two BSc degrees from ITAM: Applied Mathematics and Computer Engineering.
